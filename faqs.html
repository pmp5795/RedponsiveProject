{ FAQ'S }

Why hire – The Wedding Planners?

Planning a wedding today involves much more than one would think. Here is how Marry Me can help you plan your special day:

  • We ensure best allocation of your wedding budget
  • We use fresh international trends and yet ensure that traditional elements are not lost
  • We are professionals in this industry and use our knowledge of wedding vendors and unique design ideas to create a fabulous wedding that will be talked about for years to come
  • We are comprised of a team of young and dynamic individuals who stay abreast of upcoming trends and understand your needs totally
  • We strive to ensure that the stress is taken out of the wedding and the couple and their families can enjoy that special day
  • We enjoy taking the couple’s vision and turning it into something that is creative and unique, reflecting their style and personality
  • Most celebrities have managers, we at Marry Me become the managers of the couple from the time they avail of our services. From then on till the wedding date and sometimes even after that we manage everything for the couple.

What will you be responsible for?

We consult, plan, design and coordinate all areas of the wedding. From the venue and the stationery, décor, styling, travel arrangements, trousseau shopping and much more – we can do it all! We coordinate with vendors throughout the planning process right up to the day of the event. However, we can be involved as much or as little as you require.

How many weddings and events do you coordinate per day?

We take on only one wedding or event per day in order to give our complete attention to it.

Will hiring your wedding planning services work out to be expensive for me?

It's a myth that a wedding planner is expensive! Our services are customized to suit your budget. Our extensive vendor network ensures that you get the best deals to suit your budget. With the benefits and savings in terms of time, money and effort that you will make, you can well recover the expense of hiring us.

If I live outside of India, can you plan and co-ordinate my wedding?

Most definitely! We have successfully managed weddings where the couple has arrived in India only weeks before their wedding. We communicate regularly with the couple online and over the phone and keep them informed about every detail and ensure a fabulous wedding.

Have you managed Indian weddings outside of India?

Yes, we specialize in destination weddings! With our expertise in wedding planning and designing, as well as our increasing network of local vendors and industry partners, we can travel to any location of your choice to create your dream wedding.

If we have other wedding events – engagement ceremony, cocktail, Mehndi, Sangeetetc, can you provide services only for a particular function?

Yes we can provide services for selected functions of your choice or for all of them.

Do you plan only traditional Indian weddings?

We plan and coordinate all wedding and related events – Engagement ceremony, Sangeet, Mehndi, Church weddings, Reception, Nikaah and much more. We organize weddings of all faiths and cultures.

Does Marry Me also provide matrimonial services?

No. The Wedding Planner does not offer assistance in matchmaking. We are professional wedding planners who co-ordinate your wedding after you have found the partner of your choice.

What sizes of weddings have you coordinated?

We have coordinated intimate celebrations with just a couple of people to large scale events with guestlists running in thousands

Do I only have to work with vendors selected by you for my wedding?

We at Marry Me have a network of select reputable vendors and partners to coordinate any event. These vendors are those we have worked with over the years who provide quality products and services. However, in case you do have a preference for a particular vendor/s of your choice, we are flexible happy to work with them.

When is the best time to select your wedding planner?

We recommend that you hire your wedding planner as soon as you begin planning your wedding and before you start selecting your vendors including venue. We have however, been hired by couples that already have their venue in place and may have even finalized vendors. It’s never too late or too early!

Do you also provide matrimonial services?

No. The Wedding Planner does not offer assistance in matchmaking. We are professional wedding planners who co-ordinate your wedding after you have found the partner of your choice.

How do you charge for your wedding planning services?

We charge a one-time professional planning fee. Our initial consultation is free of charge. We then understand your requirements and create a customized proposal depending upon the services you would like to avail from us.

What other services do you offer other than wedding planning?

We plan and coordinate weddings, social events (anniversaries, baby showers, birthday parties, bridal showers etc.) and even proposals. Other than this, we offer trousseau shopping services, event and shoot styling as well as customized gifting services.
